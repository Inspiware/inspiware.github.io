Metadata-driven enterprise suite

Run finance, customer, service, and operations on a platform shaped to your business.

texerp is Inspiware's metadata-driven enterprise platform for organizations that need the breadth of modern ERP, CRM, and BPM suites without forcing every team into a rigid template. Business objects, workflows, approvals, and workspaces are defined through configuration so the operating model can reflect real requirements instead of hard-coded compromise.

Solution coverage

Enterprise coverage across core operational domains

texerp is positioned as the business-system layer for teams that need finance, customer, service, fulfillment, and operational work to run from one configurable foundation.

ERP core

Finance & master data

Manage journals, approvals, supplier and customer records, pricing, and controlled transaction workflows from one shared model.

CRM & revenue

Accounts & pipeline

Track accounts, opportunities, quotes, renewals, and commercial handoffs in workflows that stay connected to downstream operations.

HRM

People & approvals

Support employee records, onboarding, leave requests, role changes, policy approvals, and people operations from the same governed platform foundation.

Service & case

Customer operations

Run cases, investigations, escalations, queues, and task ownership with structured evidence, SLA awareness, and process visibility.

Order & fulfillment

Delivery ops

Coordinate order intake, validation, dispatch, carrier milestones, and exception handling across channels and teams.

Projects, resources & delivery

Execution governance

Plan delivery work, assign resources, track milestones, manage dependencies, and govern execution across cross-functional programs and customer-facing initiatives.

Logistics

Transport & carrier ops

Coordinate routing, carrier planning, shipment execution, depot or warehouse handoffs, delivery exceptions, and proof-of-delivery workflows beyond core order intake and fulfillment control.

Inventory & asset

Stock & maintenance

Oversee stock positions, replenishment signals, asset condition events, work orders, and maintenance-triggered actions in one operating fabric.

Enterprise capability patterns

Built for serious operational workflows

The wider Inspiware material already points to the kinds of cross-functional operational patterns texerp is built to support.

Revenue-to-cash orchestration

Coordinate opportunity, customer, order, invoice, and ERP posting flows without losing approval logic or master data discipline.

Order & fulfillment control

Standardize incoming orders, validate them against policy, enrich with product and customer context, and route them to warehouse, carrier, payment, and ERP processes.

Billing & customer 360

Unify billing events, customer history, dispute handling, and portal-facing updates in a connected operating model rather than disconnected tools.

Case management & investigations

Turn alerts, requests, or service issues into structured cases with owners, evidence, SLA tracking, escalation paths, and audit-ready outcomes.

Inventory & asset operations

Manage low-stock triggers, replenishment logic, stock reservations, asset condition events, and maintenance workflows from the same process backbone.

Governance, audit & compliance

Preserve audit trails, lineage, retention, consent, and reporting controls for regulated or control-sensitive operating environments.
