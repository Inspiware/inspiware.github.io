**Business Challenge:**  
Integrate smart meter data feeds into your billing system with real-time processing, data validation, and error handling.

**Current State:**
- Manual data imports or batch processing (daily/hourly)
- High error rates requiring manual intervention
- Delayed billing cycles
- Limited visibility into data quality issues

**TeX Solution:**
- Real-time smart meter data ingestion (MQTT/REST API)
- AI-powered data validation and anomaly detection
- Automated error handling and retry logic
- Real-time billing system updates
- Complete audit trail for regulatory compliance

**Success Metrics:**
- Process 100K+ meter readings per hour
- 99.9% data accuracy with AI validation
- <5 minute end-to-end latency
- 90% reduction in manual interventions
- Complete audit trail for all transactions

### Option 2: Renewable Asset to Trading Platform Integration

**Business Challenge:**  
Connect wind/solar farm SCADA systems to energy trading platform for real-time bidding and dispatch.

**Current State:**
- Manual forecasting and bid submission
- Delayed response to market price changes
- Limited integration between generation and trading
- Missed revenue opportunities

**TeX Solution:**
- Real-time SCADA data ingestion (OPC-UA/Modbus)
- Integration with weather forecasting APIs
- Automated pricing algorithms and bid generation
- Real-time market data integration (EPEX, Nord Pool)
- Automated dispatch instructions to renewable assets

**Success Metrics:**
- Sub-second market response time
- Automated bid generation based on real-time conditions
- 20%+ improvement in revenue capture
- Complete REMIT compliance reporting
- Zero manual interventions for normal operations

**Business Challenge:**

**TeX Solution:**

**Success Metrics:**

- 50% reduction in customer service calls
