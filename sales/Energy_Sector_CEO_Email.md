## Email Best Practices

1. **Personalize**: Research the recipient's company, recent news, initiatives
2. **Timing**: Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM local time
3. **Subject Line**: Test A/B variations, keep under 50 characters
4. **Follow-Up**: Send 2-3 follow-ups spaced 5-7 days apart
5. **Call to Action**: Make it easy to schedule (Calendly link)
6. **Mobile-Friendly**: Keep paragraphs short, use bullet points
7. **Value First**: Lead with their pain points, not your features
