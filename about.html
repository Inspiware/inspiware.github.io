Our Story

Founded in 2018, Inspiware emerged from a vision to simplify enterprise integration. Our founders, veterans of the financial services technology sector, recognized that businesses were struggling with increasingly complex system landscapes and data silos.

Today, Inspiware is a established provider of enterprise integration solutions, with our flagship TeX platform powering mission-critical operations for financial institutions, healthcare providers.
